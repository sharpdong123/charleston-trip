Morning 40 min north
The Notebook

Cypress Gardens — the swamp where they filmed the rowboat scene. Opens at 9am. You can rent a rowboat and paddle through the same water. ~$10/person admission. Allow about 2 hours for the boat ride and trails.

Cypress Gardens
Heads up: The swans were brought in for filming and aren’t there anymore — but the cypress trees, Spanish moss, and water look exactly the same. Very romantic and photogenic. Bring cash for the rowboat rental.
Early afternoon 45 min to downtown

Drive to downtown Charleston. Walk Rainbow Row and The Battery — pastel row houses and the waterfront promenade. If it’s Saturday, catch the tail end of the Farmers Market at Marion Square (closes 2pm).

Rainbow Row The Battery Farmers Market
Afternoon

Browse King Street — antiques on Lower King, boutiques on Middle King. Pop through the City Market for sweetgrass baskets and local art. Cool off at Waterfront Park by the pineapple fountain.
